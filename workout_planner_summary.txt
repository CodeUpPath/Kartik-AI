7. PRODUCT TAKEAWAYS
- The strongest part of this app is the structured exercise catalog, not the visuals alone.
- A scalable workout module needs four separate layers:
  - exercise catalog
  - plan templates
  - coaching content
  - progress / reminder system
- The app is built around short, guided, bodyweight-friendly sessions with automatic rest handling.
- Exercise cards are richer than they look in UI because they combine name, intro, video, muscles, calories, and coaching.
- The 28-day plan pattern is important. It gives users a reason to return daily.
- For a new app, the reusable idea is the data model and progression system, not the exact wording or assets.

8. WHAT TO REUSE AS PRODUCT IDEAS

- body-part and level segmentation
- short daily sessions
