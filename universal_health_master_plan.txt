CATEGORY 5: AI COACH AND INSIGHT LAYER
============================================================

Main reference direction:
- None of the studied apps alone is enough here.
- AI should sit on top of your own clean data model.

Good AI use cases:
- daily health summary
- missed-goal explanation
- next workout suggestion
- meal suggestion based on macros
- hydration nudges
- cycle-aware workout or recovery suggestions
- weekly progress summary
- natural-language search across user history

Do not do this:
- disease diagnosis
- clinical certainty claims
- fertility or pregnancy certainty claims
- medical advice without safeguards

AI data needed:
- profile
- goals
- workout history
- food logs
- water logs
- cycle logs
- body metrics
- reminders
- adherence patterns

AI output categories:
- motivation
- explanation
- recommendation
- warning
- summary

Key product lesson:
- AI should improve clarity and personalization, not pretend to be a doctor.

============================================================
CATEGORY 10: LEGAL AND PRODUCT SAFETY
============================================================

Allowed direction:
- study public app features
- study screen flows
- study category patterns
- build your own schemas
- use public and licensed datasets

Do not do:
- copy exact app UI 1:1
- reuse proprietary text, assets, or private datasets
- scrape or duplicate closed food or period databases
- present AI as medical diagnosis

============================================================
FINAL RECOMMENDATION
============================================================

Best overall strategy:
- Build one health app, not many disconnected mini apps.
- Start with workouts plus basic tracking because that gives the fastest usable product.
- Add food next because it expands daily engagement.
- Add period tracking after the privacy model and data model are stable.
- Add AI only after all core modules have clean structured data.

Best core promise for the product:
- one dashboard
- one reminder system
- one progress engine
- one AI coach
- many health categories under one system
